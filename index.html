About SCRP

Southern California Roleplay is a structured FiveM roleplay community dedicated to realism, professionalism, and immersive storytelling. Our members create authentic emergency service and civilian roleplay experiences within a detailed and organized environment. Join thousands of players in one of the most immersive roleplay experiences available.
